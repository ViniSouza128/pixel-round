Math companion

A Matemática do Projeto — full technical companion in nine languages. Covers the implicit ellipse and ellipsoid equations, three rasterization algorithms (Euclidean, Bresenham, threshold) with derivations, voxel volume, planar cuts including the 45° diagonal, spherical-coordinate camera, auto-zoom by bounding sphere, Lambertian shading approximation, and 12-tone audio synthesis.
