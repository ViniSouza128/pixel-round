Classroom lesson plans

Four-period instructional sequence for grade-12 / final-year high school classes. Each locale is adapted, not translated: every plan reflects its country's curriculum, national exam, dominant pedagogical tradition and visual culture. Includes captured figures, four-period sequence, evaluation rubric and adaptations for low-resource classrooms.

Rebuilding any locale: cd docs_aula && xelatex Plano_de_Aula_<locale>.tex (two passes for TOC + cross-refs). Requires XeLaTeX with tcolorbox, needspace, subcaption.

The pt-BR plan is the reference implementation: integrates 16 figures captured from the app, cross-references, colored links, BNCC habilities, regional crosswalk for SEDUC-SP / SEEDUC-RJ / SEE-MG / SEDUC-CE / SEDUC-AM. Other locales are adapted to their own contexts, not literal translations of the Brazilian plan.
